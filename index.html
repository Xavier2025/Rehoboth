About Us

  • We are Taking Small Steps to Make Earth Better Planet of the universe

    We, at the Redeemed Christian Church Of God aim at preaching the gospel to the whole world and winning as many souls as possible for our Lord Jesus Christ. Our prayer and wish is that every single soul on earth is saved and born again, so as to inherit the kingdom of God. We hope to save everyone from damnation and sin and bring them into a world filled with the joy of our Lord Jesus Christ. Our sunday services are as follows;

    1st Sunday: Thanksgiving Service With Daddy G.O

    2nd Sunday: Prayer Rain

    3rd Sunday: Family Sunday

    4th Sunday: Youth Sunday

    5th Sunday: Shiloh Hour
